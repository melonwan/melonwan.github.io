\section{About}
{\small
  Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs working on real-time multimodal models for human--AI
  collaboration and human--computer interaction on AI glasses and AR/VR\@.
  Led \href{https://developers.meta.com/horizon/design/design-microgestures/}{Microgesture}
  --- the gesture recognition system on Quest headsets,
  \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ztl25aRlnWE&t=1212s}{featured at the Meta Connect keynote}.
  Passionate about building intelligent agents that understand user intent from multimodal signals,
  making human--AI collaboration as natural as human conversation.
}
